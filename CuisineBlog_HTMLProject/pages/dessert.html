Badam Kheer

Sweet and creamy Indian dessert made from badam (almonds) and milk as base ingredients with ghee, saffron and cardamom powder.

Gulab Jamun

Most popular dessert consists of dumplings traditionally made of thickened or reduced milk, soaked in rose flavored sugar syrup.

Shahi Tukda

Deep-fried bread absorbs a milk sauce seasoned with saffron and cardamom, covered by fried cashews, almonds, and pistachios.

Gajar Ka Halwa

Gajar Ka Halwa is a sweet dessert pudding made by cooking the grated carrots in milk and sugar and garnished with almonds and pistachios.
