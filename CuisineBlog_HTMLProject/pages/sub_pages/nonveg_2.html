Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Cumin powder - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tbsp
  • Chilly powder - 2 tbsp
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tsp
  • Kasoori methi - 1 tsp
  • Fried onions - 2 tbsp
  • Salt
  • Sugar - 1 tsp
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • All purpose flour - ½ tsp
  • Tomato puree - 1 cup
  • Khoya - 1 tbsp
  • Cream - 2 tbsp

Method of Preparation

  • Marinate the chicken breast with yogurt, salt, chilly powder, for 3 to 4 hours. Heat butter in a pan add chicken breast, cook it for 20 minutes in a slow flame till it becomes tender and cooked well.
  • In a bowl add ginger garlic paste, cumin powder, chilly powder, coriander powder, kasoori methi powder, crushed brown onions (fried onions), salt, sugar and add water, make this into a paste, and keep it aside.
  • In a wide pan heat butter and add flour, add prepared masala paste, and mix this well, cook for 2 to 3 minutes and add grated khoya, tomato puree, cook it on a slow flame for 30 minutes. Add the cooked chicken pieces and cream and mix well, cook for 2-3 minutes and switch off the flame.
