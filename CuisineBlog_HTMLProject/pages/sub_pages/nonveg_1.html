Hyderabadi Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • pepper corns - 6
  • lime juice - 1tbsp
  • cumin and coriander pdr - ½tsp
  • cloves and green cardamoms each - 2nr
  • green chilli - 2nr
  • deep fried onion - 1cup
  • oil or (ghee) - 2tbsp
  • onions sliced - 4nr
  • salt - To Taste
  • black cardamom - 2nr
  • chopped coriander leaves - 2tbsp
  • chopped ginger and garlic - 2tbsp (each)
  • cinnamon - 2sticks
  • Basmati rice - 2cup
  • mace - 1tbsp
  • chicken medium pieces - 800grams
  • yoghurt - 2cup
  • Turmeric - to taste
  • Saffron - ½tsp

Method of Preparation

  • In a bowl take chicken, ginger garlic paste, mint, curd, green chillies, coriander leaves, pepper corns, cumin, chilli powder turmeric, salt, cumin, coriander powder and whole garam masala dry, fried onion and add oil, mix all the content and marinate for 1 hour.
  • In a vessel add water,salt and bring to boil to cook rice, once the rice is half cooked drain the water.
  • In a wide pan add marinated chicken spread at the bottom and then add half cooked rice, mint leaves, onion fried, saffron and close it with the lid and cook for 30min in low - medium flame.
