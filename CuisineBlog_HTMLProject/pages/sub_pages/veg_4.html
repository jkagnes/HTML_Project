Aloo Gobi Recipe

Ingredients

    For Onion Tomato Gravy:

  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Onion - 2 nr
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Tomato - 4 nr
  • Turmeric powder - ½ tsp
  • Chilli Powder - 2 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 2 tsp
  • Cumin Powder - ½ tsp
  • Salt - to taste

    • For Sabzi:

  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Cauliflower - 1 big, cut to bite size florets
  • Potatoes - 3 medium boiled, cut to bite size pieces
  • Tomato - 1
  • Green Chilli - 2
  • Salt - to taste
  • Cilantro – little, chopped for garnishing

Method of Preparation

  • To prepare Onion Tomato gravy, heat oil in a cooker.
  • Add chopped onions, salt, ginger garlic paste, tomato, turmeric powder, chilli powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder and saute.
  • Close the lid and cook for 6-7 whistles.
  • To prepare sabzi, in a wide pan, heat butter.
  • Add blanched cauli flower, boiled potatoes, peas(optional), roughly chopped tomato and salt and saute for 2 mins.
  • Add prepared onion tomato gravy of required amount, mix well.
  • Add water for required consistency, mix well and close lid and cook for 5-8 mins.
  • Add green chilli slit and some cilantro mix well and cook for 2 mins.
  • Finally, with chopped Cilantro Leaves and serve with Chapatti, Paratha or Rice.
