Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients

  • Khoya - 1 cup
  • All purpose flour/Maida - 2.5 tbsp
  • Cooking soda - 1/8 tsp
  • Pistachios - 1 tsp
  • Sugar - 1.25 cups
  • Water - 1.25 cups
  • Saffron - 4 strands
  • Rose Essence - 2 drops
  • Cardamom powder - 1 tsp
  • Oil - for deep fry

Method of Preparation

  • Mix the sugar, water and saffron in a bowl and make syrup.
  • Add cardamom powder and rose essence to it, mix well and keep aside.
  • Mix Khoya, Maida, Cooking soda in a bowl and add water little by little and knead to make a dough.
  • Make small sized balls and deep fry in oil, in a low flame till golden brown color.
  • When cooked, remove the dumplings from the oil and transfer immediately into the sugar syrup.
  • Repeat this till all the dumplings are cooked and added to the syrup.
  • Allow the dumplings to soak in the syrup for at least 2 hours before serving.
