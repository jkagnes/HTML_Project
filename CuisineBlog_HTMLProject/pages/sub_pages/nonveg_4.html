Tandoori Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken legs - 6 nr
  • Coriander Powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin Seed Powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala pdr - 1 tsp
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Kastoori methi (dry fenu greek leaves) - 1 tsp
  • Lemon Juice - 1 tbsp
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Peppercorns pdr - 1 tsp
  • Red Chilli Powder - 1 tsp
  • Red color - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1 tsp
  • Yogurt - 6 tbsp
  • Chat masala - 1 tsp
  • Onion - 1

Method of Preparation

  • Clean and Cut 2 or 3 long slits on each Chicken piece. Mix salt ,chilli powder and lime juice and apply all over the chicken and keep aside for 15 minutes.
  • Prepare marination mixture by mixing yogurt, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, salt, pepper, kasturi methi, turmeric, garam masala, cumin powder, corriander powder, lime juice all together to a paste and finally red color powder.
  • Apply the marination mixture to the Chicken throughly and between all the slits and add little oil over the mixture.
  • Preheat your oven to 425 degree Fahrenheit. Cook for 20 to 35 minutes till the chicken is tender.
  • Garnish with sliced onions and lime wedges and also can be heated on griddle and for additional taste can sprinkle chat masala on top, little sliced onion and lime juice.
