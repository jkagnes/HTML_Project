Vindaloo Recipe

Ingredients

  • Red chilli - 5 nr
  • Cumin seeds - ½ tsp
  • Whole garam masala (each) - 2 nr
  • Mustard seeds - ½ tsp
  • Pepper corns - 4-5
  • Garlic cloves - 5 nr
  • Ginger - 1 inch
  • Vinegar - 2 tbsp
  • Coconut Milk - 3 tbsp
  • Sugar - ½ tsp
  • Lamb - 500 grams
  • Salt - to taste
  • Meat tenderizer (green papaya paste) - 2 tsp
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Onion - 1 nr

Method of Preparation

  • Soak the following ingredients for 30 mins and grind to paste: red chilli, cumin seed, whole garam masala, mustard seeds, pepper corns, garlic cloves, ginger, vinegar, sugar and coconut milk.
  • In a bowl, take the meat, add salt and meat tenderizer and marinate for 1 hour.
  • To the marination, add the ground paste in step 1 and again marinate for almost 4 hours.
  • In a wide pan, heat oil and add the marinated meat and saute for 5 mins, add finely chopped onion and cook in low flame for 30-45 mins till meat becomes tender and oil oozes out in the gravy.
