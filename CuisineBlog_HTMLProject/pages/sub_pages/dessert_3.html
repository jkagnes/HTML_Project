Shahi Tukda Recipe

Ingredients

  • Milk - ½litre
  • Sugar - ½cup
  • Bread - 4-6 slices
  • Cardamom powder - 1 tsp
  • Rose Essence - 2 drops
  • Saffron - few strands
  • Ghee - for deep fry
  • Mixed nuts - little for garnishing

Method of Preparation

  • Boil milk till it reduces to half of its volume to thick consistency.
  • Add saffron, cardamom, and rose essence to the milk.
  • Prepare thick sugar syrup by boiling sugar with little water.
  • Cut the crusts off from the bread slices and quarter the slices.
  • Deep fry the bread in ghee, in low flame.
  • Soak the fried bread in sugar syrup for few minutes.
  • Arrange the soaked bread in plates and pour the thickened milk mixture over it.
  • Finally garnish with chopped nuts like cashew, almonds and pistachios.
