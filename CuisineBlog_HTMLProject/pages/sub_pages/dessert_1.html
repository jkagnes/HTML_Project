Badam Kheer Recipe

Ingredients

  • Almonds - 1/4 cup
  • Milk - 2.5 cups
  • Saffron - few strands
  • Cardamom powder - 1 tsp
  • Sugar - 1/4 cup
  • Ghee - 2 tbsp

Method of Preparation

  • Blanch the almonds and peel the skin and grind to paste with required milk.
  • Soak the saffron strands in 1 tbsp warm milk.
  • Boil the 2.5 cups milk and then add the grounded almond paste, sugar, cardamom and soaked saffron strand.
  • Stir the content often around 8 to 10 mins in medium flame.
  • Fry some almond silvered in ghee and add it to the kheer.
