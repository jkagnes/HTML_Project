Gajar Ka Halwa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8-10 medium carrots, grated
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • ½cup sugar
  • 2 cups skimmed milk
  • ½tsp cardamom powder
  • 8-10 cashewnuts chopped
  • 8-10 almonds, chopped
  • 5-7 raisin
  • ½cup crumbled khoya

Method of Preparation

  • In a heavy bottom pan, add ghee, when heated add chopped nuts and raisins and fry for 2-3 mins
  • Add grated carrot to it and cook for 4-5 mins.
  • Add milk to it, allow to cook in a low flame till the carrot completely absorbs it, it take 15-20 mins.
  • Add sugar and mix well, cook for 4-5 mins.
  • Add cardamom powder and khoya and mix well
  • Cook for 10 mins till the mixture thickens and ghee oozes out.
  • Finally garnish with chopped nuts.
